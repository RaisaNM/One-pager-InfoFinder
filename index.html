InfoFinder

Lost or you feel you want some change on how your visitors navigate around your museum? The Pathfinder is the device for you to make your visitors enjoy their stay!

What is the InfoFinder

The Inforfinder is an item that chooses for you an exhibition if you feel you don't want to follow the basic route or want something new.

  1. Click on the button to let the InfoFinder choose the exhibition.
  2. Once the light stops, that's the chosen destination the visitor can go!
  3. Furthermore, information will be shown on the InfoFinder's screen.

Why you should buy the InfoFinder?

  • Interactiveness with navigation will look fun for the visitors.
  • New and old visitors can create their own navigation through the museum.
  • Can be used any time for new and old exhibitions.
